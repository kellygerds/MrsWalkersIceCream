Mrs. Walker's has been providing high-quality products since we opened our doors in 1986. Every day, we strive to provide you with friendly service and the best ice cream there is. We pride ourselves at being the top product with great value in our industry. We genuinely care about our customers and want to insure them with a great all around experience with our service and our products. Our products and services are not limited, so feel free to ask us what it is you are looking for and we will be sure to help you any way we can. Our menu is full of countless sundae variations. Choose between numerous flavors, over 50, and plentiful amounts of delicous toppings. From hot fudge to fresh fruit toppings, enjoy your ice cream as delightful as possible. We provide featured items such as milkshakes, cakes, blend-ins, splits, sundaes, floats, italian ices, gelatis, frozen novelties, and more! Stop by at our main location in Forked River, NJ or enjoy our award winning ice cream in other locations including Toms River, Beachwood, Manahawkin, and Lakehurst.
